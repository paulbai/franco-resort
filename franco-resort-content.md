## About Section

**Label:** Our Story

**Headline:**
> Built from the Shore, *With Heart*

**Body:**
> Franco Resort is more than a place to stay — it's a labour of love. Built from scratch by Franco and Florence, a Sierra Leonean/Italian family, the resort sits directly on a pristine tidal inlet along one of Sierra Leone's most beautiful stretches of coastline. Rebuilt after the war and lovingly refined over the years, it remains one of the most personal and characterful escapes on the entire peninsula.
>
> With Mediterranean-inspired architecture, panoramic views of the peninsula mountains, and the sound of the ocean at every turn, Franco's is the kind of place that stays with you long after you've left.

## Beach & Activities Section

**Label:** Experience

**Headline:**
> Sun, Sand & *The Deep Blue*

**Body:**
> The resort sits on its own private beach — a sheltered tidal inlet where children can play safely at the water's edge while parents unwind with a chilled glass of wine. At low tide, the beach opens up into a wide, quiet stretch of unspoiled coastline, often completely deserted. It's a place to slow down, breathe deep, and feel the world simplify.

**Activities:**
- Private beach on a tidal river inlet
- Access to the wider Sussex Beach (short walk at low tide)
- Diving school run by Franco on-site
- Fishing trips arranged by the owners
- Beach huts and lounge chairs
- Stunning peninsula mountain views and Atlantic sunsets
- Easy day trips to River No. 2 Beach, Lakka Beach, and Bureh Beach
