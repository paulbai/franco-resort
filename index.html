Our Story

Built from the Shore, With Heart

Franco Resort is more than a place to stay — it's a labour of love. Built from scratch by Franco and Florence, a Sierra Leonean/Italian family, the resort sits directly on a pristine tidal inlet along one of Sierra Leone's most beautiful stretches of coastline.

Rebuilt after the war and lovingly refined over the years, it remains one of the most personal and characterful escapes on the entire peninsula.

With Mediterranean-inspired architecture, panoramic mountain views, and the sound of the ocean at every turn — Franco's is the kind of place that stays with you long after you've left.

Experience

Sun, Sand & The Deep Blue

The resort sits on its own private beach — a sheltered tidal inlet where children can play safely while parents unwind with a chilled glass of wine. A place to slow down, breathe deep, and feel the world simplify.

Private Beach

A sheltered tidal river inlet with beach huts, lounge chairs, and a wide stretch of unspoiled coastline at low tide — often completely deserted.

Diving School

Franco runs the on-site diving school personally. Explore the crystal-clear waters of the Atlantic off the Freetown Peninsula.

Fishing Trips

Arranged by the owners themselves, head out into the Atlantic for an authentic deep-sea fishing experience.

Peninsula Views

Stunning panoramic views of the peninsula mountains and unforgettable Atlantic sunsets from every vantage point.

Beach Hopping

Easy day trips to River No. 2 Beach, Lakka Beach, and Bureh Beach — some of West Africa's most beautiful shores.

Atlantic Sunsets

Watch the sun dip below the horizon from the restaurant terrace or the sand — with a cold drink in hand and nowhere to be.
